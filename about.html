Activities

Throughout my time in high school I have had many amazing
experiences through a variety of clubs and activities. I
was a memeber of the marching band as well as the indoor
color guard all four years of highschool. During my junior
and senior years I was one of the color guard section leaders
as well as one of the indoor team captains.

Since fourth grade I have played viola in the school orchestra.
I have loved playing the instrument since I began and was a part
of auditioned orchestra during my sophomore, junior, and senior years.
During my freshman year I also was given the opportunity to play viola
in the pit for our spring musical.

Additionally, our school started a LINK program during my junior year.
After being selected as a Link Crew Leader I was tasked with working closely
with a group of incoming freshamn to help make their transition to
highschool sooner. This gave me a deeper appreciation for the positive
culture at my school as well as the compassion and patience of my peers that
I worled alongside.
